Initialize the infinite line charge with its equation and charge density. 

   The line equation is written in the form ``ax + by + c = 0``, where a is ``x_coef``, b is
   ``y_coef``, and c is ``offset``.
Parameters
x_coefThe coefficient on x in the equation of the line (a).
y_coefThe coefficient on y in the equation of the line (b).
offsetThe offset in the equation of the line (c).
charge_densityThe charge density, in C/m.
Exceptions
RuntimeErrorCannot have both the x and y coefficient equating to zero.
